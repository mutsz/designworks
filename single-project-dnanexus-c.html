Method

How to design a solution to approach all the issues?




To be considerate and thorough on all elements

I start with a Style Guide Structure.

To capture the conceptual ideas, I began with a Styleguide structure. To illustrate this structure, please think of it as a ball. From the inner to outer layer of the ball, four components are included:

1. Fundemental elements
2. UI Components
3. Interaction pattern
4. What users see.



If you were a chef, fundemental elements are like ingredients of your meal, they should be fresh and present your style well. UI components are your dishes, they are reasonably match with one another and highlight your ingredients. Templates are the tablewares or places that you choose to present your dishes.

To enhance interaction design

Some Principles in mind




* 1. Are the interface elements a reasonable size to interact with?

Note it is a platform for scientist who are comfortable with large amount of data.

* 2. Are familiar format used?

The web platform should follow the conventional UI design. While at the same time, since most users had already gotten used to the platform, preserving most parts of the funtionality is still important.

3. [After] What feedback does a user receive when an action is performed?


4. [Before] Do elements give users clue about how it functions and what will happen?


5. What is user's main task in a page? In what way could the interaction assist them?
